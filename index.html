About me

Where data meets
the human story

I'm a statistical researcher who got into the field because I couldn't stop asking why. Why do people vote against their interests? Why does the same policy work in one place and fail in another?

My tools are R and Python. My real interest is in the gap between what we can measure and what actually matters. I write about politics, philosophy, and the occasional inconvenient truth — not always in that order.

Currently writing, coding, and asking inconvenient questions.
